What We Do

Three pillars of expert consultancy for supplement brands

We work exclusively with supplement brands and manufacturers — providing the independent expertise needed to operate compliantly, build trust, and grow with authority.

📋

Regulatory Compliance

Comprehensive guidance on FDA DSHEA requirements, 21 CFR Part 111, structure/function claims, NDI notifications, and label compliance across US and international markets.

🔬

Supplement Integrity

Independent verification of ingredient identity, purity, and potency. Third-party testing program design, COA review, adulteration risk assessment, and certification pathway support (NSF, USP, Informed Sport).

📈

Brand & Market Strategy

Competitive analysis, positioning strategy, go-to-market planning, and science-backed brand storytelling to differentiate your products in a crowded, skeptical marketplace.
