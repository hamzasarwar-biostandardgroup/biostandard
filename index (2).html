What We Do

Four ways we turn clinical rigor into market authority

We work exclusively with supplement brands and manufacturers — delivering independent, evidence-based expertise that protects your reputation, strengthens retailer relationships, and earns lasting consumer trust.

📊

BioStandard Audit™

Our flagship proprietary assessment — an objective, data-driven report that benchmarks your supplement against published clinical evidence and the competitive landscape. Clearly organized and visually compelling, the BioStandard Audit™ identifies exactly where your product meets or falls short of doses and standards supported by research. An indispensable tool for retailer conversations, buyer presentations, and internal product development.

Flagship Offering
🏅

BioScore™ Consumer Report

A consumer-facing version of your audit — distilled into a transparent, easy-to-understand score from 0 to 100. Issued by BioStandard Group as an independent body, the BioScore™ gives your customers verifiable proof of quality and integrity. In a market saturated with unsubstantiated claims, third-party validation from a credentialed, unaffiliated source is one of the most powerful trust signals a brand can offer — and one of the clearest drivers of purchase confidence.

Regulatory Compliance & Claims Substantiation

Expert review of your product labels, structure/function claims, and marketing materials benchmarked against FDA DSHEA requirements, FTC guidelines, and published clinical literature. We help you substantiate what you say with science, and identify language that may draw regulatory attention — without acting as legal counsel or making guarantees of outcome. Think of us as your scientific second opinion before anything goes to print or market.
